## Stage 1: Retrieval Criteria

Retrieval Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE；用於建置候選集）：

R1. Search sources include top-tier machine learning venues and audio research venues (e.g., major ML/audio conferences and journals).
R2. Search sources include arXiv to capture non-peer-reviewed but timely work.
R3. Candidate works are restricted to publication years 2020 onward.
R4. Candidate works are filtered by publication venue and citation information.
R5. Candidate works are screened by the final keyword logic used in the paper.
R6. arXiv candidates are additionally constrained by per-year citation cutoffs (Table A.2):
    - 2020: citations ≥ 28
    - 2021: citations ≥ 22
    - 2022: citations ≥ 32
    - 2023: citations ≥ 90
    - 2024: citations ≥ 13
    - 2025: citations ≥ 1
R7. If keyword change alters results by more than 100 papers, authors review a random sample to decide whether to keep/remove that keyword.
R8. Keyword search refinement continues iteratively until successive rounds yield no new candidate keywords.

## Stage 3.5: Final Selection and Inclusion（可程式化核心）

Paper is eligible iff all three conditions hold:

1. Paper pertains to preference learning.
2. Paper applies within the audio domain.
3. Paper is not a survey/review.

## Stage 2: Screening Criteria

Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）：

I1. Preference-learning component exists: the paper includes comparisons/rankings of audio items
  (ranking, A/B comparison, pairwise preference), or explicitly defines preference signals that are
  used for learning.

I2. Audio-domain application is present. For multi-modal works, this includes any paper where audio is a
  core modality.

I3. Paper is not a survey/review article.

Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）：

E1. Preferences are used only for evaluation, with no learning/optimization/training component.

E2. Paper is not preference learning (fails the operational definition below).

E3. Paper is not an audio-domain work.

E4. Paper is a survey/review article.

### §3.5 對「Preference learning」與「Audio」的操作化定義

**Preference learning（偏好學習）**

- Counted as preference learning when the paper involves ranking or A/B comparisons of two or more audio clips.
- If only numeric ratings are provided (e.g., 1–5 MOS), they are counted only when they are explicitly converted into rankings/pairwise preferences (e.g., 5 > 3 implies A > B).
- Preference signals can be from human judgments or synthesized preferences.
- Even without explicit pairwise comparisons, papers with an RL training loop for an audio model are included (if optimization is driven by preference-derived signal).
- Papers where preference signals are used only for evaluation are excluded.

**Audio domain（音訊領域）**

- Multi-modal work is included when it includes audio content (e.g., audio/speech/music signals), even if combined with other modalities.

## Screening Question Set（給抽取型 agent；請只提供本段，不要提供上面的 criteria）

> 目標：只做**資訊抽取 + 定位**，不要做是否納入/排除的結論。

### Q1. Preference learning component
1. Paper 是否明確呈現 ranking / A/B comparison / pairwise preference / preference learning 的原文？（可多段）
2. 偏好資料來源是 human judgments 還是 synthetic generation？
3. 若使用 numeric ratings（如 MOS）：有無明示轉成 ranking / pairwise preference？請摘錄可支持原文。
4. 若沒明示 comparisons：是否存在 RL training loop / reward model / preference-driven optimization（例如 RLHF / DPO / KTO）？請摘錄 evidence。

### Q2. Preference usage (learning vs evaluation)
1. 原文中偏好訊號主要用於哪個環節？（training / optimization / fine-tuning / evaluation）
2. 若只用於 evaluation 請摘錄對應句子；若用於 learning 請摘錄 training/optimization evidence。

### Q3. Audio-domain evidence（含 multi-modal）
1. 摘錄 paper 的 input/output 是 audio/audio-text/music/speech 等的句子。
2. 若為 multi-modal，請同時列出 audio 是否出現及其他模態。

### Q4. Survey / review / overview 檢核
1. 是否自稱 survey / review / systematic review / meta-analysis / overview？（逐字原文）
2. 若未明示，摘錄最能支持非 survey 的論文型態（例如方法實作、實驗設計描述）。

### Q5. Table A.1 taxonomy 的 evidence signaling
1. 根據 Q1–Q4 輸出以下欄位，但僅回報 evidence-based signal（不做結論）：
   - signal_audio = {present/absent/unclear}
   - signal_preference_learning = {present/absent/unclear}
   - signal_survey = {yes/no/unclear}
   - signal_eval_only = {yes/no/unclear}
2. 每個欄位至少 1–2 段 evidence_quotes。

### Q6. Metadata / 可直接程式化欄位
1. publication_year（或 arXiv year）
2. venue / source（top venues / arXiv / cited）
3. 是否 arXiv（true/false）
4. citation_count（若 metadata 有；否則 null）

### Q7. Citation gate（若實作為 stage1 filter）
1. 列出該 paper 的 year 與 citation_count。
2. 按 Table A.2 對應 year 的 cutoff，回報是否達到門檻。
