## Stage 1: Retrieval Criteria

Retrieval Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE；用於建立候選集）：
R1. Systematic review conducted following PRISMA guidelines.
R2. Database includes Web of Science.
R3. Database includes Scopus.
R4. Database includes IEEE Xplore.
R5. Database includes ACM.
R6. Database includes ScienceDirect.
R7. Search query focus: intersection of two key concepts — NLP and BPM (Business Process Management).
R8. Search query uses synonyms and subfields for NLP and BPM as listed in Table 1 of the paper (Table 1 not fully reproduced in the extracted criteria text).

## Stage 2: Screening Criteria

Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）：
I1. Full research article published in a peer-reviewed conference or journal.
I2. Full text is available.
I3. Written in English.
I4. No restriction on time period (publication year is not used as an exclusion).
I5. Primary research article presenting original research contributions (not secondary research).
I6. Specifically covers the use of NLP for process extraction from natural language text.
I7. Explicates a concrete method.
I8. Describes experiments to empirically validate the method.

Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）：
E1. Not a full research paper (e.g., poster, short paper, abstract-only publication, report, thesis, or book chapter).
E2. Not published in a conference or journal (e.g., workshop, discussion forum, white paper, or technical report).
E3. Not peer-reviewed (including preprints).
E4. No full text available.
E5. Not written in English.
E6. Secondary research article (e.g., literature review, meta-analysis, or comment).
E7. Does NOT specifically cover NLP for process extraction (e.g., focuses on process redesign, matching, prediction, sentiment analysis, individual labels, or generating natural text from processes).
E8. Only superficially discusses the applied method.
E9. Does not describe experiments to validate the method.

## 可程式化判定的條件（metadata-only；不需要 LLM 判定）

- 出版時間（Publication time）：
  - Stage 2: I4（No restriction on time period）→ 永遠為 TRUE（不做 year-based exclusion）
- Peer-reviewed 與否（Peer review status / venue type）：
  - Stage 2: I1（peer-reviewed conference or journal）→ `is_peer_reviewed == true` 且 `venue_type ∈ {conference, journal}`
  - Stage 2: E3（Not peer-reviewed, including preprints）→ `is_peer_reviewed == false` 或 `publication_status == preprint`
- Paper 長度 / 出版型態（Pages / full paper vs short/poster/abstract-only）：
  - Stage 2: E1（Not a full research paper: poster/short/abstract-only/report/thesis/book chapter）→ `publication_type` / `page_count` 可初步判定（但 poster/short 有時需 venue 元資料）
- Open-access / Full-text availability：
  - Stage 2: I2（Full text is available.）→ `full_text_available == true`
  - Stage 2: E4（No full text available.）→ `full_text_available == false`

## Screening Question Set（給抽取型 agent；請只提供本段，不要提供上面的 criteria）

> 目標：只做**資訊抽取 + 定位**，不要做是否納入/排除的結論。

### Q1. 出版型態與同儕審查（conference/journal & peer-reviewed）
> 多半可由 metadata 程式處理，但仍請抽取 paper 內可見證據（若有）。
1. paper 是否為 conference paper / journal article？請摘錄 bibliographic line 或 footnote。
2. paper 是否明示 peer-reviewed（或明示在 peer-reviewed venue）？  
   - 若無明示：回答「未明說」（不要自行推論）。
3. paper 是否為 poster / short paper / abstract-only / report / thesis / book chapter？  
   - 若有任何詞出現，請逐一摘錄；無則答未明說。

### Q2. Full text availability / Open access
1. paper 是否可取得 full text（open access / arXiv / publisher PDF）？  
   - 若 paper 內有敘述，摘錄；若無答未明說。

### Q3. 語言（English）
1. paper 是否明示 written in English？有則摘錄；無則答未明說。

### Q4. 研究類型（Primary research vs secondary research）
1. paper 是否自稱 literature review / systematic review / mapping study / meta-analysis？  
   - 有則摘錄；無則答未明說。
2. 若不是 review：請摘錄 Abstract 中描述 “we propose/present a method” 的一句原文（若有）。

### Q5. 任務定義（NLP for process extraction from natural language text）
1. 摘錄 paper 中一句最能代表任務的原文：是否在做 process extraction（從自然語言萃取 business process / process model）？
2. Input 是什麼 natural language text（requirements / manuals / emails / policies…）？摘錄原文。
3. Output 是什麼 process 表示（process model / BPMN / activity sequence / Petri net / event log / labels…）？摘錄原文。

### Q6. 方法是否「具體且可執行」（concrete method）
1. paper 是否提供具體方法描述（algorithm/pipeline/model）？  
   - 請摘錄一段最能代表 “our approach/method” 的原文。
2. 若 paper 只是在概念性討論沒有方法細節：請摘錄能支持的原文（若無則答未明說）。

### Q7. 是否有實驗/實證驗證（empirical validation）
1. paper 是否描述 experiments / evaluation / empirical study？  
   - 有則摘錄能支持的一段原文（含 dataset/metrics/baselines 任一即可）；無則答未明說。
2. 若 paper 有實驗表格（Table）或 evaluation section：請列出章節/表格編號並摘錄一小段附近原文（不解釋）。
