## Stage 1: Retrieval Criteria

Retrieval Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE；用於建立候選集）：
R1. Primary information source for retrieval: Semantic Scholar’s indexed papers.
R2. Query construction uses primary terms (to match papers in text generation) and secondary terms (to focus on specific characteristics, e.g., training).
R3. Temporal filter: exclude papers from 2016 or older (i.e., focus on publications from 2017 onwards).
R4. Automatic filtering uses Semantic Scholar influential citations as an impact proxy for ranking/filtering.
R5. To even out yearly coverage, sample the top 5 papers per query and year by influential citations.
R6. For papers up to (and including) year 2021: exclude papers with fewer than 2 citations (as stated in the pipeline figure).
R7. For years 2022 and 2023: relax restrictions and do not remove papers with less than two influential citations.
R8. Auxiliary papers may be added if they are referenced by other relevant works and contain the source method applied by those works (crediting original ideas).
R9. Auxiliary papers may be added by specifically exploring Google Scholar for subfields of text generation from regular discussions.
R10. Relevance annotations (titles/abstracts) are recorded and released publicly in the accompanying GitHub repository.

## Stage 2: Screening Criteria

Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）：
I1. Manually assessed candidate works are within years 2017 to 2023 (titles and abstracts manually assessed for relevance).
I2. Relevance target: text generation (manual relevance judgment based on titles and abstracts).

Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）：
E1. Paper is too specific for the scope of this review (e.g., multimodality).
E2. Work employs studies to another field of study (e.g., medicine).

## Screening Question Set（給抽取型 agent；請只提供本段，不要提供上面的 criteria）

> 目標：只做**資訊抽取 + 定位**，不要做是否納入/排除的結論。

### Q1. 是否屬於 text generation（範圍抽取）
1. 摘錄 Abstract/Introduction 中一句最能代表 paper 任務/研究範圍的原文（優先含 “text generation”, “natural language generation”, “generation” 等）。
2. paper 的主要任務是什麼？（例如 summarization / translation / dialogue generation / data-to-text / story generation / code generation…）  
   - 請用作者原文支持（可多段）。

### Q2. 發表年份（2017–2023 範圍判定所需資訊）
> 可由 metadata 程式處理，但仍請抽取 paper 內可見年份（若有）。
1. 摘錄 paper 顯示的發表年份/日期（若 paper 內明示）；否則答「以 metadata 為準」。

### Q3. 排除訊號：是否為多模態/過於特定子領域（只抽取，不下結論）
1. paper 是否使用非文字模態（image/audio/video/sensor）作為 input 或 output？  
   - 請逐項列出模態並附 quote。
2. 若 paper 以某個非常狹窄的 subdomain 為主（例如特定 biomedical/clinical generation）：
   - 摘錄能支持其 domain-specific 範圍的一句原文。

### Q4. 排除訊號：是否把研究應用到「另一個領域」（例如 medicine）
1. paper 的應用領域是什麼？（例如 medical/clinical/healthcare/legal/finance…）  
   - 請摘錄作者原文（可多段）。
2. 若 paper 明確屬於 medicine/clinical：請摘錄最能支持的一句原文。
