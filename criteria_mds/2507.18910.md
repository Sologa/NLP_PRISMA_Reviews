## Stage 2: Screening Criteria

Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）：

I1. Relevant to RAG or closely related baselines: clearly integrates a retriever with a generative
  language model OR uses retrieval to supply context to a generator.

I2. Centers on knowledge-intensive tasks (e.g., open-domain QA, fact-checking, knowledge-grounded
  dialogue, or other tasks benefiting from external document retrieval).

I3. Source is peer-reviewed or otherwise reputable (e.g., presented at major AI/NLP venues such as
  ACL/NeurIPS/ICML/EMNLP, or from recognized industrial R&D labs such as IBM/Meta/NVIDIA).

I4. Preprints are included (to broaden the scope of the survey).

I5. English language (only English texts included).

Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）：

E1. Solely retrieval OR solely generation: focuses strictly on IR techniques or purely generative models
  without explicit retrieval-augmented integration.

E2. Minimal discussion of RAG: retrieval+generation is only peripheral/superficial and lacks substantial
  results or analyses.

E3. Non-substantive publication: very short abstract, publicity note, or material without verifiable
  methodology.

E4. Non-English paper.
