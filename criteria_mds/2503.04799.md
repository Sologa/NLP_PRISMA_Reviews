## Stage 2: Screening Criteria

Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）：

I1. Focuses on direct Speech-to-Speech Translation (S2ST) (i.e., S2ST is the focus of the review).

I2. Published between 2016 and 2024.

I3. Relates to state-of-the-art work in machine learning regarding S2ST.

I4. Covers direct S2ST techniques that avoid intermediate text representations.

I5. Studies on application of models like Translatotron 2, UnitY, or other neural network structures
  (neural S2ST models).

Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）：

E1. Cascade models regarding S2ST that concern conversion of intermediate texts (i.e., uses intermediate
  text representations).

E2. Papers that present machine translation for text-only or audio-only tasks without mentioning S2ST.

E3. Experiments are scanty (insufficient empirical evaluation).

E4. Relies solely on outdated datasets.
