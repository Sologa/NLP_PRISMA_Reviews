## Stage 2: Screening Criteria

Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）：
I1. Primary goal/task: classifying dysarthria patients based on severity levels.
I2. Assesses intelligibility as a factor corresponding to severity levels.

Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）：
E1. Review paper.
E2. Survey paper.
E3. Does not contain the main keywords in the title and abstract.
E4. General chapter related to motor speech disorders (not specifically dysarthria severity classification).
E5. Focuses on other disorders such as Dysphagia.
E6. Focuses on other disorders such as Dementia.
E7. Tools or software for rehabilitation or therapy (rather than severity classification research).
E8. Automatic recognition systems or identification of dysarthria (rather than classifying severity levels).
E9. Studies about the impact of specific features of dysarthric cases (rather than severity-level classification).
E10. Binary classification of dysarthria (rather than severity-level classification).
