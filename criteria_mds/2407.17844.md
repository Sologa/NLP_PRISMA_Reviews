## Stage 1: Retrieval Criteria

Retrieval Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE；用於建立候選集）：
R1. Search performed in April 2024.
R2. Database includes Google Scholar.
R3. Database includes SpringerLink.
R4. Database includes IEEE Xplore.
R5. Year filter: 2020 onwards.
R6. Search term includes: “speech processing Parkinson”.
R7. Search term includes: “speech recognition Parkinson”.
R8. Search term includes: “speech classification Parkinson”.
R9. Additional filter: discard studies related to neuroimaging (neuroimaging-focused PD diagnosis literature discarded).
R10. Manual identification: read title, abstract, and keywords, looking for terms such as deep learning, Transformers, CNNs, LSTMs, transfer learning, and deep features.

## Stage 2: Screening Criteria

Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）：
I1. Uses deep learning (DL) methods for Parkinson’s disease classification using speech data.
I2. Uses speech (vocal features) as the primary data modality.
I3. May examine both vocal features and linguistic features (allowed).

Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）：
E1. Duplicate study record (duplicates removed across databases).
E2. Uses traditional methods such as GMMs (Gaussian Mixture Models).
E3. Uses traditional methods such as HMMs (Hidden Markov Models).
E4. Uses machine learning without deep learning (ML without DL).
E5. Focuses on neurodegenerative diseases in general rather than specifically on Parkinson’s disease.
E6. Literature review paper (other literature reviews discarded).
E7. Absence of deep learning (no DL).
E8. Uses types of data other than speech.
E9. Relies on non-speech-related features such as gait.
E10. Relies on non-speech-related features such as handwriting.
E11. Neuroimaging-based studies (discarded during retrieval/filtering).

1. 摘錄 Abstract/Introduction 中一句最能代表 paper 任務的原文（需含 Parkinson 或 PD）。

### Q2. 主要資料模態（speech / vocal features）
1. paper 的主要資料來源是否為 speech / voice / vocal features？  
   - 有則摘錄；無則答未明說。
2. 若同時使用 linguistic/text features：請摘錄原文並說明它與 speech 的關係（例如從 speech transcript 得到）。

### Q3. 方法類型（Deep Learning vs traditional / ML without DL）
1. 列出 paper 使用的模型/架構名稱（CNN/LSTM/Transformer/Deep features 等），每個附 quote。
2. 若 paper 使用 GMM 或 HMM：請摘錄原文並列出用途（feature modeling / classifier）。
3. 若 paper 使用傳統 ML（SVM/RF/kNN 等）但沒有 DL：請摘錄原文（不要評論）。

### Q4. 排除訊號：非 speech 資料或其他特徵（gait/handwriting/neuroimaging）

4. 是否使用其他非 speech 的生理訊號（EEG/EMG 等）？（若有請列出）
