R5. Evidence scope for analysis: main text + included appendices only; external supplementary materials are not examined.

## Stage 2: Eligibility / Screening Criteria (atomic, screenable conditions)

### Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）

I1. Full research paper (long-form); not an extended abstract/book chapter/other non-full-paper form.
I2. Topic is **generative audio models**, with a generative audio model/application as the **primary focus**.
I3. The paper either:
    - introduces a generative audio model/application, OR
    - analyzes/discusses generative audio models as its main focus.
I4. Output domain is **entirely audio** (the model’s final output is in the audio domain).
I5. Temporal window: submitted/published within **2018-02-01 to 2023-02-01**.
I6. Evidence scope for coding/analysis includes main text + appendices inside the paper.

### Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）

E1. Not actually about audio as the central topic or output (e.g., text, vision, choreography, or another non-audio focus).
E2. Speech-to-text (ASR / transcription) where output is **text** (treated as text generative model for this review).
E3. Generative models whose final output is **video** (visual domain conflation).
E4. Non-generative output (e.g., classification/prediction models).
E5. Generates something *in addition to audio* as a primary output (e.g., gestures/images/video) — violates “output entirely audio”.
E6. Proposes only a metric / incremental methodological improvement without actually discussing audio outputs or use cases (screening/full-text exclusion reason explicitly used).
E7. Overly broad “all-encompassing” papers that discuss generative models for text/vision in addition to audio, instead of focusing on audio.
E8. Extended abstract / book chapter / other non-full-paper publication forms.
E9. External supplemental materials outside the main body/appendix (not examined; exclude if eligibility depends on them).

## Screening Question Set（給抽取型 agent；只做資訊抽取 + 定位 + quote，不做判定）

### Q1. Output 是否為 audio 且「只輸出 audio」
1. 摘錄 paper 對 output 的一句原文（audio/music/speech/sound vs text/video/…）。
2. 若 paper 提到輸出包含 gestures/images/video：逐項摘錄原文並記錄位置。

### Q2. 是否為 generative model（vs classification/prediction）
1. 摘錄 paper 是否明示 generate/synthesize/produce audio 的原文；若是 classification/prediction，摘錄能支持的原文。
2. 摘錄 paper 中最能代表 “generative model/application” 的一句。

### Q3. 主題是否以 generative audio models 為 primary focus
1. 摘錄 Abstract/Intro 最能代表主題聚焦的原文。
2. 若 paper 只是提出 metric / incremental improvement：摘錄能支持 “metric/evaluation only” 的原文（若有）。

### Q4. Speech-to-text / ASR 排除訊號
1. paper 是否做 speech-to-text / transcription？有則摘錄，並標明 output 是否為 text。
2. 若是 text-to-speech：摘錄能支持 output 為 audio 的原文。

### Q5. Temporal window & publication type
1. 摘錄 paper 內可見的年份/出版資訊（沒有就說以 metadata 為準）。
2. 是否有 “extended abstract / book chapter” 等字樣？有則逐一摘錄；無則答未提及。
