## Stage 1: Retrieval / Search-space definition (screenable rules only)

R1. Databases searched (candidate sources): Web of Science, Scopus, IEEE Xplore, ACM, ScienceDirect.
R2. Search queries target the intersection of two concepts: NLP and BPM; synonyms/subfields and the concrete query strings are given in Table 1.

## Stage 2: Eligibility / Screening Criteria (atomic, screenable conditions)

### Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）

I1. Full research article in a **peer-reviewed conference or journal**.
I2. **Full text available**.
I3. Written in **English**.
I4. Primary research (original contribution; not secondary research).
I5. Specifically covers **use of NLP for process extraction from natural language text**.
I6. Explicates a **concrete method**.
I7. Describes **experiments** to empirically validate the method.
I8. No publication-year restriction (i.e., do not exclude by year).

### Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）

E1. Not a full research paper (poster/short/abstract/report/thesis/book chapter).
E2. Not published in a conference or journal (workshop/discussion forum/white paper/technical report).
E3. Not peer-reviewed (including preprints).
E4. No full text available.
E5. Not written in English.
E6. Secondary research (literature review/meta-analysis/comments).
E7. Not specifically NLP-for-process-extraction (includes: process redesign/matching/prediction, sentiment analysis, targeting individual labels instead of natural text, generating natural text from processes).
E8. Only superficially discusses an applied method.
E9. Does not describe experiments to validate the method.

## Screening Question Set（給抽取型 agent；只做資訊抽取 + 定位 + quote，不做判定）

### Q1. 任務定義（Process extraction from natural language text）
1. 摘錄一句最能代表 paper 任務的原文（Abstract/Intro/Problem statement）。
2. Input 是什麼 natural language text？摘錄原文。
3. Output 是什麼 process representation（BPMN/DMN/Petri net/…）？摘錄原文。

### Q2. 方法是否具體（concrete method）
1. 摘錄 paper 描述 “our approach/method/pipeline/model” 的一段原文。
2. 若 paper 只概念性討論：摘錄能支持的原文（若無則答未提及）。

### Q3. 是否有 experiments / empirical validation
1. 摘錄 paper 中最能證明有 experiments/evaluation 的原文（可包含 dataset/metrics/results）。
2. 若沒有：答「未提及實驗」，不要推論。

### Q4. 研究類型（primary vs secondary）
1. paper 是否自稱 systematic review/survey/mapping/meta-analysis？有則摘錄；無則答未明說。
2. 若非二手研究：摘錄一句 “we propose/present” 類的 contribution 句（若有）。

### Q5. 語言/全文/同儕審查（metadata-friendly but still quote if present）
1. paper 內是否明示 English / full text / peer-reviewed venue？逐項摘錄（沒有就寫未明示）。
