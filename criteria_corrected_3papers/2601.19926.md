## Stage 1: Retrieval / Candidate Identification Rules (search-space definition)

These are *retrieval rules* (define how candidates are collected), not eligibility screening.

R1. Cut-off date for inclusion: **up to July 31, 2025**.
R2. Targeted Google Scholar keyword search combining:
    - “syntactic structure/knowledge”
    - AND “LLMs/LMs/transformer”
R3. Snowballing using the six existing reviews on the topic:
    - (a) all references cited by the reviews
    - (b) some references in those references
    - (c) most papers that cite the reviews (via Google Scholar)
R4. If a review had >100 citations: only check **top 30–40%** (sorted by Google Scholar “relevance”); otherwise check **all** citations.
R5. Relevance assessment protocol: title first; if unclear → abstract; if still unclear → full text.

## Stage 2: Eligibility / Screening Criteria (atomic, screenable conditions)

### Inclusion Criteria（全部必須為 TRUE）

I1. The study is about **Transformer-based language models** (TLMs) (not primarily non-transformer; not primarily encoder–decoder MT architectures).
I2. The study **empirically assesses or analyzes syntactic structure / syntactic knowledge** in TLMs.
I3. The publication type is one of:
    - peer-reviewed journal article
    - peer-reviewed conference paper
    - archived preprint

### Exclusion Criteria（全部必須為 FALSE；任一 TRUE 即排除）

E1. Focuses primarily on **non-transformer architectures** (e.g., RNNs/LSTMs), OR focuses on **encoder–decoder architectures** (particularly MT-oriented).
E2. Analyzes Transformer LMs but **does NOT empirically assess/analyze syntax**.
E3. **No clear empirical component** (e.g., position paper, survey/review).
E4. Publication type is **not** a peer-reviewed journal/conference paper, and **not** an archived preprint (explicitly implies excluding blog posts and theses/dissertations).

## Screening Question Set（給抽取型 agent；只做資訊抽取 + 定位 + quote，不做判定）

### Q1. 模型架構（Transformer vs non-transformer / encoder–decoder MT）
1. 列出 paper 研究/實驗的主要模型架構名稱（逐一附原文 quote）。
2. paper 是否主要在做 encoder–decoder MT（或主要任務是 MT）？若有，摘錄能支持的原文。

### Q2. 研究焦點是否為 syntax / syntactic knowledge（作者自述 + 任務/現象）
1. 摘錄 Abstract/Intro 一句最能代表研究焦點的原文（需包含 syntax/syntactic/grammar 或具體現象）。
2. 列出 paper 檢驗的 syntactic phenomena / tasks（逐項附 quote）。

### Q3. 是否有 empirical component（實驗/評估證據）
1. paper 是否有 experiments/evaluation/results？有則摘錄一段最能支持的原文（含 dataset/metric/result 任一即可）。
2. 若 paper 自述為 position paper / survey / review：摘錄最能支持的原文。

### Q4. Publication type / venue evidence（用於 E4）
1. 摘錄 paper 的 venue / bibliographic line（journal/conference/preprint）。
2. 若是 thesis/dissertation/blog post：摘錄能支持的原文或頁首資訊；若無則答「未提及」。

### Q5. 不屬於 screening 的 retrieval 規則（僅供追溯）
1. paper 是否被納入是因為 review-snowballing（cited-by / reference）？若 paper 內或資料中可見，請摘錄/記錄來源欄位。
